Mision La Muralla Hotel

A Thematic Hotel in Queretaro to live the Mexican Revolution.

Hotel Mision La Muralla Amealco is the only thematic hotel in Mexico, where you can relive the era of the Mexican Revolution and be part of an adventure like no other, with excellent quality and service that characterizes Hoteles Mision.

The Hotel is located in Amealco, in the south of Queretaro. Location: 25 minutes away from San Juan del Rio, 45 minutes from the city of Queretaro, and 2 hours from Mexico City.
