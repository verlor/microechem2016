About


You are cordially invited to the MicroEchem 2016 / Energy Storage Discussions 2016, that will be held in Hotel Misión La Muralla, Amealco de Bonfil, Queretaro, Mexico from November 6th to 9th, 2016.

Since 2013, there has been an inherent tradition in Microechem (Summer School in Molecular Electrochemistry) for the dissemination of topics related to Molecular electrochemistry, with particular emphasis on the techniques used for the molecular understanding of electrochemical processes. For previous editions of the School see: https://sites.google.com/site/microechem2015/

In 2016, the event remains similar in essence and organization, but it is intended to cover recent advances in energy storage systems, as a result of its promising and novel research areas, which have spurred most of the current investigation conducted in electrochemistry. To this concern, it will be focused on the dissemination of both experimental and theoretical background in “Electrochemical Energy Storage” and will provide an overview on techniques and methods used to fabricate, characterize and model these electrochemical devices. The meeting will include Tutorial Lectures from renowned speakers, invited lectures and ad-hoc contributions either oral or in poster format considering the following topics of interest, but not limited to: (1) Quantum Mechanical and Multi-Scale Modelling, (2) Synthesis of Materials and (3) Macroscopic Modelling.

MicroEchem 2016 / Energy Storage Discussions 2016 is a joint meeting between Microechem and Energy Storage Discussions , which was the first forum of analysis of different aspects of energy storage, organized in Mexico. The 2014 edition of ESD was very successful.

Please consider that the number of participants is limited to 40; therefore, a “first come-first served” basis is applied. For information on registration and payment procedures, send an email to energysd@ifuap.buap.mx.
